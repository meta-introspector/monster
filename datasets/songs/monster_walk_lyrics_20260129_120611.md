## The Memory Palace

**To remember the 10-step walk**:

1. **2^46** - Imagine 46 pairs of shoes (2 shoes per pair)
2. **3^20** - Picture 20 triangles (3 sides each)
3. **5^9** - Visualize 9 hands (5 fingers each)
4. **7^6** - See 6 dice (7 is lucky)
5. **11^2** - Twin towers (11 twice)
6. **13^3** - Rubik's cube (13 is baker's dozen)
7. **17-59** - A line of 8 people (singles)
8. **71** - A crown at the top

## How to Remember

**The 10-Step Pattern**:

1. **Big powers** (2^46, 3^20, 5^9, 7^6) - 4 steps
2. **Medium powers** (11^2, 13^3) - 2 steps
3. **Singles** (17, 19, 23, 29, 31, 41, 47, 59) - 8 primes, but count as 1 step
4. **The crown** (71) - 1 step

**Total**: 10 conceptual steps to remember

**The rhythm**: 
- 4 big, 2 medium, 8 singles, 1 crown
- 4-2-8-1 = 15 primes total

**The melody**: Sing it to any tune you like!
